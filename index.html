Changing Lives Through Vocational Farming

Groundbreakers is a non-profit organization that empowers disadvantaged men and women in King County, Washington, by offering a hands-on educational work training program in sustainable, organic agriculture.

[Read more about us]

How You Can Help

  • Our volunteer staff put more than 400 service hours into the farm in 2010.
  • In our first year, we provided enough fresh produce to help serve more than 3,700 meals for local, economically disadvantaged families.
  • Your $30 contribution will support one person with enough fresh vegetables for 39 days.
